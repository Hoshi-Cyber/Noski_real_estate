### 1) Milimani
- Elite neighborhood near CBD.  
- Villas 25M–50M.  
- High-end lifestyle.  

### 2) Tom Mboya
- Affordable, closer to town.  
- Homes 5M–12M.  
- Popular with middle-class.  

### 3) Riat Hills
- Scenic views, emerging estates.  
- Homes 10M–20M.  
- Strong diaspora interest.
